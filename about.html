About DLight

Designers, writers, and storytellers. Working as one.

DLight Creatives is a Lagos-based creative agency for ambitious brands. Founded in 2024, we bring design, story, and strategy together to help businesses stand out and grow.

Our story

How we started.

DLight Creatives was founded in September 2024. I was working remotely at the time, and noticed the gaps in how creative agencies in Nigeria communicated with their clients and managed their work.

As more international and local agencies started expanding into Nigeria, I saw a real need for trusted partners. Professionals who not only deliver creative excellence, but also bring structure, clear communication, and a deep understanding of the African market.

That is why we exist. At DLight Creatives, we help clients ensure quality delivery from creative agencies and partners, while bridging cultural and operational gaps to make collaboration smooth.

Through this work, I have watched an emerging shift in the African creative industry. A movement that values intentionality, professionalism, and innovation. That is the energy that drives us forward.

Our mission is clear: to lead the next chapter of African creativity. To empower bold ideas. And to shape the future of the industry, one project at a time.

Chisom Mmika Founder and Chairperson, DLight Creatives

How we work

Our process, end to end.

Six steps from the first conversation to the final hand-off. Built so you always know what is happening, and what is next.

  1. 01

    Briefing and Understanding

    We sit with you to understand the problem, your goals, your audience, and your business. No assumptions, just questions.

  2. 02

    Strategy and Planning

    We map out the approach, the scope, the timeline, and the team we will need. You see the plan before we start building.

  3. 03

    Concept and Creative Direction

    We develop ideas, present directions, and align with you on the creative path before any production begins.

  4. 04

    Production and Execution

    We build, write, design, and film. With check-ins along the way so nothing surprises you at the end.

  5. 05

    Review and Refinement

    We collect your feedback and refine until the work is right, not just done. Iteration is built into the process.

  6. 06

    Delivery and Follow-up

    Final assets are delivered cleanly and clearly labelled. And we stay close after the hand-off to support the launch.
