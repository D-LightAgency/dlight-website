Before you ask

Common questions.

What kind of projects do you take on?

We work on brand identities, video production, graphic design, marketing campaigns, scriptwriting, and consultation. Most of our projects last between two and twelve weeks, depending on scope.

Do you work with clients outside Nigeria?

Yes. We are based in Lagos, but we work with clients across Africa and beyond. Remote collaboration is normal for us, and we are set up for it.

How quickly do you reply?

Within 24 hours, Monday to Friday. If you reach out on a weekend or public holiday, expect to hear back first thing on the next working day.

How much does a project cost?

It depends on the scope. After our first call we send a written proposal with scope, timeline, and price. Smaller projects start lower, larger campaigns scale up. We will be clear about cost before any work begins.
